    Multiplication

    Long multiplication, also known as the column method, is a method in which you multiply two numbers which are difficult to multiply easily in your head. For example, we can easily find the product of 55 × 20 by multiplying 55 by 2 and then adding a 0 at the right most place of the answer. 55 × 2 = 110 and 55 × 20 = 1100.But, many a time finding the product is not this easy. At such times we use the long method of multiplication.
